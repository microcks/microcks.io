We are beyond thrilled to announce a major milestone in our journey: **Microcks has officially been accepted as an Incubating project** by the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF)!

{{< image src="images/blog/microcks-incubating-banner.jpg" alt="image" zoomable="true" >}}

Moving from the **Sandbox** to the **Incubating** stage is a massive achievement. For the Microcks community, this isn't just a new label; it is a formal validation of our project's maturity. Reaching this level means the CNCF Technical Oversight Committee (TOC) has thoroughly reviewed and confirmed our real-world adoption, solid governance models, and rigorous security posture.

> For platform teams and enterprises evaluating tools for production-grade API mocking and testing, **Incubating status** represents the credibility threshold required to rely on a tool at scale.

## A Year of Explosive Growth

Our momentum leading up to this announcement has been record-breaking. In the latest [**CNCF Projects Velocity report**](https://github.com/cncf/velocity#current-reports) (1/1/2025 - 1/1/2026), Microcks ranked within the **top [65 of over 236 projects](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1-NNsw5AH9SFLld6Vu76CSIhgSi0GB-0GuQFFYfrw6_g)**, a testament to the sheer energy of our ecosystem.

This velocity is reflected in our 2025 metrics:

* **Global Adoption**: We saw a massive surge in usage, with annual container image downloads jumping from **850k in 2024 to 2.5+ million in 2025**: more than 3x growth year-over-year.  
* **2026 Headstart**: The momentum isn't slowing down; we have already reached over **1.3 million downloads** since January 2026 alone.  
* **Community Depth**: Our contributor base grew to **167 active individuals** representing **35 different organizations**, ensuring diverse perspectives drive our roadmap.  
* **Trusted by Industry**: We now have **34 public adopters**, with **13 new organizations** joining the list in 2025 to help demonstrate Microcks' sustainability in real-world business contexts.
